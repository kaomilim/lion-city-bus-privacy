Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

Lion City Bus Simulator ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Summary: We use Google AdMob for ads and Mapbox for maps. We do not collect or store your personal data on our servers. Location data (if permitted) is only used locally to center the map.

Information We Collect

Information Collected Automatically

When you use our app, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent in app, crash logs
  • Location Data: If you grant location permission, we access your location solely to center the map on your current position. This data is not stored or transmitted to our servers.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Financial information
  • Social media accounts
  • Contacts or address book

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Ad interaction data

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Mapbox

We use Mapbox for map display and satellite imagery. Mapbox may collect:

  • Anonymized location data
  • Map usage telemetry

For more information, see Mapbox Privacy Policy.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Analyze app usage patterns
  • Fix bugs and technical issues

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Google AdMob for advertising purposes
  • Mapbox for map functionality
  • Analytics providers to improve our service

Data Retention

We retain usage data for up to 24 months. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is rated for Everyone (PEGI 3).
